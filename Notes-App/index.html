Welcome to QuickNotes!

QuickNotes is a simple and intuitive web application designed to help you organize your thoughts, jot down ideas, and keep track of important information effortlessly. Whether you're a student, professional, or just someone who loves staying organized, QuickNotes is here to make your life easier.

How to Use:
  • Create New Note: To create a new note, simply click on the "New Note" button. This will open a blank note where you can start typing your thoughts.
  • Edit Note: Click on any existing note to open it for editing. You can then add, delete, or modify text as needed.
  • Save Note: Your notes are automatically saved as you type. There's no need to worry about losing your work!
  • Delete Note: If you no longer need a note, simply click on the delete icon to remove it from your list.
